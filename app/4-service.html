What is a AquaGold Fine Touch?

AquaGold Fine Touch is a patented gold-plated, hair-thin needle system that painlessly delivers custom bioactive compounds into the skin at the ideal 600 micron depth. Solutions used with the AquaGold Fine Touch system include micro-Botox, micro-hyaluronic gel, antioxidants, retinols, growth factors and vitamins.

What Should I Expect after AquaGold Fine Touch?

After your AquaGold Fine Touch treatment at Adam and Eve Medical Aesthetics in Scottsdale, results are typically visible in three to seven days. Most patients report a reduction in the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, less discoloration of the skin, reduced appearance of acne scars and healthier, more radiant looking skin. Results will typically last approximately three to four months. The procedure can be repeated as often as every one to three months. Completing several treatments throughout the year often enhances and extends results.

Who is AquaGold Fine Touch for?

Patients wishing to ease the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles or improve the overall texture of their skin are often good candidates for AquaGold Fine Touch. Those with deeper wrinkles may need to explore other treatment options.

The use of certain medications and some health conditions may make AquaGold Fine Touch an unsuitable medical aesthetics treatment for some patients. One of our highly trained aestheticians will review your medical history and analyze your skin at a pre-treatment consultation. This information as well as a conversation about your concerns and goals will help us determine if AquaGold Fine Touch is the right choice for you.

What Does an AquaGold Fine Touch Treatment Consist of?

AquaGold Fine Touch is a safe and effective microneedling procedure. There is no downtime, and you can resume normal activity immediately after your appointment. While there is only a minimal chance of bleeding during the procedure, we do advise that patients stop taking any non-prescription blood thinner medications or supplements in the one week prior to your appointment (i.e.: aspirin, NSAIDS, vitamin E). Do not stop taking any prescription blood thinners without consulting with your prescribing physician, but please do let us know about them at your initial consultation.

AquaGold Fine Touch appointments typically take less than 30 minutes and are pain-free. No anesthetic is required. Arrive in our facility about 15 minutes ahead of your scheduled appointment to allow time for questions and any last minute paperwork. The medical aesthetician will perform the treatment and discuss post-treatment protocol.Some redness and sun sensitivity are normal and will usually subside quickly. Contact Adam and Eve Medical Aesthetics in Scottsdale if you have any questions or concerns.

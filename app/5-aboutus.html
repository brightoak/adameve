Under the direction of Dr. Carl Sonder, Adam & Eve Medical Aesthetics provides innovative medical grade aesthetic and cosmetic procedures. In effort to remain current in our rapidly changing and ever-growing field, our physicians receive ongoing training in the latest advancements in aesthetic medicine, so they may offer a broad selection of procedures producing natural-looking results without the downtime and strain of traditional surgery. With access to our highly trained medical staff of physicians and certified technicians and the latest cutting-edge technologies in aesthetic medicine, our patients experience rejuvenating and corrective care that transforms and restores the face and body.

In addition to being Botox experts, our facility provides aesthetic treatments for both the face and body including: IPL Photofacial, Dermal Filler, Ultherapy, Vein Removal, Skin Rejuvenation, Vaginal Laser Rejuvenation (FemTouch), SculpSure (permanent fat reduction) and more.

Since 2012, Regency Medical Aesthetics and Adam & Eve Medical Aesthetics have achieved Allergan’s Black Diamond status, placing their practitioners among the top 1% of all medical aesthetic practices in the U.S. to facilitate Botox, Juvederm, and Voluma injections. Allergan states that “The certification is awarded to leaders in the field of medical aesthetics and demonstrates their commitment to innovation, excellence and the highest level of patient care.” Regency Medical Aesthetics and Adam & Eve Medical Aesthetics have also been named a 2015 Ultra Premier Ultherapy Provider for having the best treatments in the Scottsdale area! Our patients have had great success with Ultherapy, the only non-invasive procedure FDA-approved to lift skin on the neck, chin and brow.

We welcome patients to enjoy the serene environment of warm earth tone interiors, elegant ambiance and private concierge driver service. Please visit us for a complimentary patient consultation at our Scottsdale location Adam & Eve Medical Aesthetics (480) 771-2819 and we can design a treatment program specifically catered to your goals.
